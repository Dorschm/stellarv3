# 3D Models Needed (.glb)

All units currently use placeholder Three.js procedural geometries. Each needs a proper .glb model.

**Target poly count: 30-50 triangles per model (extreme low-poly style).**
**All models must be flat shaded with hard edges — no smooth shading, no curves, no textures.**
**Origin at model center. Forward direction is +Y (nose/front points toward positive Y).**

---

## Mobile Units

### 1. Assault Shuttle

**Meshy prompt (height: 2.5m):**

> Low-poly sci-fi dropship. Flattened hexagonal prism hull (6-sided cross-section, 12 faces) stretched lengthwise. Front two faces beveled inward at 30° forming a nose wedge. Two trapezoidal wing stubs angled 25° downward from mid-hull (1 quad each, 4 triangles total). Rear face: pentagonal thruster recess inset 15°. Silhouette: compact angular brick with stubby fins.

### 2. Battlecruiser

**Meshy prompt (height: 4.5m):**

> Low-poly sci-fi capital ship. Elongated octagonal prism hull (8-sided cross-section, 16 faces). Front 4 faces taper inward at 20° to a sharp 2-face bow point. Rear widens 40% past midsection. Two rectangular nacelle boxes (6 faces each) on rear flanks, splayed 10° outward. Dorsal triangular fin along rear third of hull, 45° pitch. Dagger profile from the side.

### 3. Trade Freighter

**Meshy prompt (height: 3.0m):**

> Low-poly sci-fi cargo freighter. Wide rectangular box hull (6 faces), 2:1 width-to-height ratio — heavy and flat. Front face chamfered 35° on top and bottom edges forming a blunt cockpit wedge (4 triangles). Two cubic engine pods (6 faces each) at rear corners, offset 15° outward. Raised trapezoidal cargo ridge along top center (4 faces). Wider than long from above.

### 4. Frigate (Engine)

**Meshy prompt (height: 2.5m):**

Front: flat hexagonal coupling plate recessed inward with square socket indent (2 triangles). Raised rectangular dorsal plate (2 triangles) along top half.

### 5. Frigate (Carriage - Empty)

Front and rear: flat square coupling plates (2 triangles each) centered vertically. Two internal X-pattern cross-braces (4 triangles). Reads as an empty lattice container.

### 6. Frigate (Carriage - Loaded)

**Meshy prompt (height: 3.0m):**

Turret barrel on front-upper face: triangular prism (5 faces) angled 15° upward from horizontal. Two trapezoidal armor plates (2 triangles each) flanking the turret.

### 15. Point Defense Array

**Meshy prompt (height: 2.5m):**

> Low-poly sci-fi missile launcher array. Inverted hexagonal pyramid base (6 triangular faces), wide top tapering to point at 55° face angle. On flat hexagonal top: four square-prism missile tubes in 2x2 grid (4 visible faces each, 16 triangles), angled 5° outward from vertical, open at top (no caps).

### 16. Orbital Strike Platform

**Meshy prompt (height: 3.5m):**

> Low-poly sci-fi weapons platform. Dodecagonal disk base (12-sided, 24 triangles top/bottom) with 12 visible faceted side faces. Central hexagonal prism cannon barrel (6-sided, 12 faces) mounted at center, angled 25° from vertical toward front. Barrel tip: open hexagonal bore (no cap). Rectangular targeting sensor plate (2 triangles) on barrel front face.

### 17. Spaceport

Two trapezoidal pylon arms extending inward from opposite sides, angled 10° downward, tapering from wide at ring to narrow at tip (2 triangles each). Two raised triangular fin markers at 12 and 6 o'clock (2 triangles each).

### 18. Foundry
